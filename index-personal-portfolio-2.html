About Me

————

I'm Vincent Londez. New York based Creative Graphics Designer/3D Artist. I've worked as a Motion Designer, and 3D Artist at LA Kiyal and Bioyot, collaborating with clients such as Slack, Zoom and KOCO. Then led a Design team as a Creative Director at Markotia

I've always tried to give equal importance to story-telling and composition, balancing the two. Tried to transform the cgi work as a new way to express oneself. Also I'm mimini periores vitae.
